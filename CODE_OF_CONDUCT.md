# Code of conduct

Weeklight is committed to a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free
community for everyone.

Contributors should:

- communicate with empathy and technical clarity;
- welcome different experience levels and perspectives;
- give actionable feedback about the work, not the person;
- respect privacy and avoid sharing another person's data; and
- accept moderation decisions made to protect the community.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sustained disruption, and publication of
private information are not acceptable. Repository maintainers may edit,
remove, or reject contributions and participation that violate these standards.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Reports will be
handled as confidentially as practical.
